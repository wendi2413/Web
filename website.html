Introduction

This is my website on a couple of my favourite animes. My liking for this genre started last year, in august 2022, when my friend insisted we watch an episode of Haikyu (which to this date is still my favourite anime). But my journey started in june 2022 (I think) when I watched an episode of Naruto with my brother. I really liked it at the time, but through comparison with other titles, it has gone down a bit on my list of preference... It will always have a special place in my heart as the first I've ever watched though.

Featured Animes

For this website I'll go over some specific animes I watched, specifying different elements about them without order. They're all accessible on the menu at the top, or by clicking their names here.
  • For a volleyball centered sport anime, I have my all-time favourite Haikyu!!
  • For a shonen where the protagonist is literally slaying (demons), we have Demon Slayer.
  • For a shonen that kinda reminded me of Naruto with the vocabulary they used for the powers (jutsu), but who turned out very different from my expectations in a good way, I have Jujutsu Kaisen to present to you.
  • For a cute and comic shonen, of which you'll get attached to all its characters, Spy X Family has a special place in my heart and list of recommandations.
