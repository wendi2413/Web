Jujutsu Kaisen

When I decided to start watching JJk, I was aware of the opinions about it going around. This anime was very popular when it released, just like its manga. Many stated that it's "overhyped", even though a lot people love it. It ended up surpassing largely my expectations, and I really love the anime now. I do agree it might be a little bit overhyped, but I stand that it's still a very good story. I really started liking the story when the older students showed up and fell in love while watching the Sisters School Exchange Event. I will definitively watch the JJK0 Movie. I love many characters, but I feel like my favourites aren't the same level as Haikyu's. I think the story was moving really fast and I didn't have the time to really bond with a specific character. Among the tops, I have Itadori, Gojo, Inumaki, Nanami, and Panda.

UPDATE: After watching JJK0, my favourite character has now become Inumaki and Yuta has also taken up a special place in my heart. In my opinion, JJk0 is very well made and I love the plot and story line. Inumaki's really made an impression on me and I have made it my favourite character :). Yuta is the nicest person ever and his character development really made me happy. Overall, I'm really immersed in their universe now.

Some information about JJK season 2: it's gonna be released on July 2023!! It's probably gonna contain 24-26 episodes seperated into 2 cours of 13 episodes. It will cover the The Shibuya Incident Arcstarting from the 8th volume in the manga series. Another good news is that the manga series is still ongoing, the latest volume published being the 19th on April 4th 2022.
